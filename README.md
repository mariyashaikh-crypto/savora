🍽️ Savora — Food Demand & Waste Optimization Platform

Savora AI is an AI-powered web application designed to help restaurants reduce food wastage and optimize daily operations.
It analyzes key inputs such as day, weather, and demand patterns to predict food requirements and provide actionable insights.

---

🚀 Features

- 🤖 AI Demand Prediction
  Estimates daily food requirements

- 📊 Industry Insights
  Provides hotel analytics including logistics and daily waste (kg/day)

- 🌍 Savora Optiserve
  Connects hotels with nearby NGOs for efficient food donation

- 🧠 AI Insights
  Generates smart recommendations for waste reduction

- 🎯 Impact Simulator
  Visualizes potential savings and social impact
